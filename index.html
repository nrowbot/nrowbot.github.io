What is a SOC?

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is typically a team of professional Security Engineers that monitor, detect, and respond to malicious activity against a business. A SOC protects a company and its customers' data.

What makes SOC in a Box different?

SOC in a Box finds and integrates the opensource tools that best fit the recommendations and requirements specified in leading security frameworks for you! These automated tools then run and notify you of security threats. All of this is put together in one easy to start package!

Who is this for?

Small businesses make up 99.9% of all U.S. businesses! With a significant amount of those being online, cyber security is a critical consideration to ensure your business and customers stay safe. SOC in a Box helps to make sure you have the security you need without necessarily having all the technical expertise or expense another solution would require.
